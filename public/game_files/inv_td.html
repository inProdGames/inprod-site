Inverse Tower Defense

Inverted Productions > Our Games > Inverse Tower Defense


Inverse Tower Defense offers a new perspective on traditional tower defense games. The player creates his or her own wave of enemies for the AI to defend against. After deciding the order of the enemies, a normal tower defense screen shows up. Unlike normal tower defense games, the player is now trying to keep the attackers alive and the AI is placing the turrets. Money is awarded for every second the player stays alive. There is also a two player game mode in which one player creates waves of enemies and the other defends the towers with turrets.
