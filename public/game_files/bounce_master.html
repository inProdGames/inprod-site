Bounce Master

Inverted Productions > Products > Bounce Master


Bounce Master is a unique game in which you must bounce a ball to the end of the level. The catch is that you only control a floating paddle that you can bounce the ball against to aim it. You have no control over the ball or the ground. Different surfaces increase or decrease the speed of the ball. If the ball falls into a pit or loses all its bounce, you fail the level.
